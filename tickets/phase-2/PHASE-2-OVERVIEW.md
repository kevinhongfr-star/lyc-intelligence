---

## What This Phase Does

Builds the webinar infrastructure — the central engagement engine of the flywheel. 24 webinars over 12 months (2/month), each with its own page, registration flow, and connection to a specific diagnostic instrument.

## The Flywheel

```
Free Webinar → Free Diagnostic → Paid Workshop → Paid Programme → Council (inner circle)
```

Webinars are the entry point. Every webinar page links to:
1. The related free diagnostic (e.g., governance webinar → BRIDGE diagnostic)
2. Related paid products (programme/workshop)
3. Newsletter signup
4. Soft CTAs (Phase 6)
