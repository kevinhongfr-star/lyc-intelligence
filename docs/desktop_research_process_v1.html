Slide 2 · Source Landscape
Step 1 · Where the data lives

China's talent data is fragmented

Five complementary sources. No single platform is complete — cross-source is the only path to a real picture.

Liepin
猎聘 · Senior focus
Strong on senior management and mid-career professionals. Most MNC-friendly of the Chinese platforms.
Zhilian
智联招聘 · Volume
Largest resume database. High volume, mid-tier focus. Weaker on leadership and confidential searches.
Zhipin
BOSS 直聘 · Active
Active job-seeker platform. Best for current titles, recent transitions, real-time salary bands.
LinkedIn
领英 · Global
Cross-border roles, MNC presence, expat profiles. Weaker on local Chinese firms and confidential roles.
+ Open Web
IR · News · WeChat
Company IR pages, regulatory filings, industry news, WeChat articles. Captures org changes and context.
Why 5 sources? Each platform indexes a different slice of the market. Liepin captures senior MNC, Zhipin captures active job-seekers, Open Web captures org changes. Combined, they triangulate a complete view.

Step 3 · Why LYC analysts validate every data point

AI builds the draft. Our analysts make it real.

Pure LLM extraction has known failure modes. LYC validation is the QA gate that turns raw AI output into decision-ready intelligence.

Layer 1
AI extracts
Fast, broad, multilingual. Surfaces candidates the analyst would not have found at manual speed. Reads 5 sources in parallel.

Layer 2
LYC desktop verification
Analyst re-searches, fact-checks dates, verifies tenure claims, traces every profile back to its original source. Reads the org chart and the people.

Slide 5 · Output & Handoff
Step 4 · What the engagement receives

A complete picture, ready for assessment

Three structured artifacts, each source-linked and confidence-scored. They become the input layer for the LYC evaluation engine.

Artifact 1
Org chart
Nested tree of business units, levels, and headcount per target company. For 8 comparator companies, fully resolved to named individuals.
8 comparators · 5 BUs · 5 levels · ~200 nodes per company
Artifact 2
Talent profiles
Per-individual profile cards with title, tenure, scope, education, key projects, source confidence. Pre-scored on the 5-criteria matrix.
50–200 per company · 5-criteria scored · source-linked
Artifact 3
Cross-company pool
Aggregated view of comparable talent across all 50 target companies. Filterable by BU, level, geography, and seniority band. Exports to CSV.
~5,000 profiles aggregated · filterable · CSV export (Phase 1.5)
Handoff to assessment
Desktop research is the input layer. The LYC evaluation engine consumes it for full assessments, interviews, and shortlisting.
Example: Codelco China CPO Search — desktop research surfaces the long-list. Assessments then rank, validate, and present the final 3–5.
